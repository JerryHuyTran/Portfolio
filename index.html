Who Is Jerry?

Hello, I am Gia Huy — but everyone usually calls me Jerry. I am driven by a constant desire to learn, create, and turn ideas into meaningful solutions. Technology, languages, and personal growth are at the center of my journey. I enjoy exploring new challenges, stepping outside my comfort zone, and continuously expanding my knowledge through both academic and real-world experiences.

Currently, I am studying Japanese Information Technology at Da Nang University of Science and Technology, where I combine my passion for programming, artificial intelligence, and innovation. Beyond technical skills, I value curiosity, creativity, meaningful connections, and experiences that help me grow as both a student and an individual.

One of my major goals is to achieve the JLPT N2 certification by December 2027 while continuing to strengthen my expertise in software development and emerging technologies. I am particularly interested in building digital products, leveraging AI to solve practical problems, and creating solutions that make a positive impact.

In the long run, I aspire to work in an international environment, especially in Japan, explore different cultures, and continue learning throughout my life.
